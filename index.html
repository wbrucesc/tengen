quick guide

Liberties

The empty points touching a stone or group — its breathing room (green here). Fill a group's last liberty and it's captured.

Atari & capture

A group down to one liberty is in atari — Tengen pulses it orange. One more enemy stone on that last point (the orange ring) captures it.

Eyes & living

An eye is an empty point your group fully surrounds. A group with two real eyes can never be captured — it's alive. Making two eyes is how groups survive.

Territory

Empty points surrounded by only your stones are your territory. Most area (plus komi, White's compensation) wins.

Ko

You can't play a move that repeats the whole board — so you can't instantly recapture in a one-stone back-and-forth. Play elsewhere first.
